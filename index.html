How do I read the blog monitor dashboard? +

The blog monitor tracks all KEEP posts from the 2026 audit and flags them by risk level:

  • High danger Post lost significant traffic or ranking — needs immediate action
  • Medium danger Declining — worth prioritising soon
  • Watchlist Worth keeping an eye on
  • Stable No action needed

Use the Monday email and the #blog-monitor Slack message to decide which posts to update first each week.

Link: lwoue-collab.github.io/redpoints-blog-monitor
What does the publishing updates dashboard show? +

It tracks how many blog posts have been published or updated each month, with:

  • Clicks, impressions, CTR and position change per post
  • Whether updated posts are recovering traffic

Navigate between months using the arrows. Data refreshes every Monday — not live, at most 7 days old.

Link: redpoints-blog-monitor/dashboard.html
